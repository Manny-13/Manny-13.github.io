About

My name is Manuela Panuccio. I'm 24 years old, born in Ottawa, Ontario, and I'm currently enrolled at Algonquin College for Interactive Media Design.

My interests are in graphic design and photography, but I do enjoy learning about the UX/UI, coding, and video aspects of the program. I've learned how to use multiple Adobe programs, such as Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Experience Design, Premiere, After Effects, and Audition. I've also learned how to code by using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP.

When I'm not at school, you can find me hanging out with friends, watching movies, reading, or writing.
